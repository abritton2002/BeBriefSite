Everything You Need to Brief

Professional-grade tools designed for VFR pilots

Route Planning & Go/No-Go

Plan your route with real-time weather analysis. Get instant go/no-go recommendations based on current conditions, forecasts, and your aircraft performance.

  • Multi-waypoint route planning
  • Automatic weather analysis
  • Flight category indicators
  • ETA-based forecasting

Live Weather & METARs

Real-time METAR and TAF data from NOAA Aviation Weather Center. See VFR, MVFR, IFR, and LIFR conditions at a glance with decoded, human-readable summaries.

  • Real-time METAR updates
  • TAF forecasts decoded
  • Winds aloft data
  • Flight category color coding

Performance Calculator

Takeoff and landing performance calculations for 50+ aircraft types. Automatically adjusts for temperature, altitude, wind, and runway conditions.

  • Takeoff & landing distances
  • Density altitude calculations
  • Weight & balance tracking
  • CG envelope visualization

Smart NOTAMs

No more parsing through walls of text. NOTAMs are automatically categorized, prioritized, and filtered. Critical alerts for runway closures and TFRs are highlighted.

  • Categorized by type
  • Priority-based sorting
  • TFR alerts highlighted
  • Plain English summaries

Airspace Awareness

See TFRs, restricted areas, and special use airspace along your route. Never fly into airspace you shouldn't be in.

  • TFR visualization
  • Restricted & prohibited areas
  • MOAs and alert areas
  • Route conflict detection
