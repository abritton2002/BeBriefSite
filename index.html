Your briefing flow

Plan. Brief. Evaluate. Fly.

The product story is simple: every screen exists to help a pilot move from route intent to source review to a clearer preflight decision.

Readable product screens

Every screenshot has a job.

No washed-out backdrops, no random wall of app photos. Each iPhone frame explains one part of the pilot workflow.

Route setup

Build the advisory brief from real flight inputs.

Departure, destination, waypoints, timing, and speed all sit together before the brief is generated.

  • Mission details stay visible before the request.
  • Route edits can lead into a refreshed brief.
  • Inputs stay tuned for pilots instead of generic trip planning.

Source review

Weather and hazard categories are easy to scan.

Briefing sections separate weather, NOTAMs, alerts, SIGMETs, PIREPs, and winds so pilots can inspect the right source quickly.

  • Critical and caution counts are visible before drill-in.
  • Each category keeps source detail discoverable.
  • The route remains attached to the brief context.

Route context

Map review shows the flight path and surrounding airspace.

Route overlays, weather layers, TFR context, and chart views make the planned path easier to understand before the pilot opens individual details.

  • Route-loaded map views keep orientation fast.
  • Sectional context supports visual inspection.
  • Hazard review does not bury the route itself.

Aircraft readiness

Performance numbers stay attached to the trip.

Takeoff, landing, fuel, W&B, runway margins, and V-speeds are shown in the same preflight flow as the advisory brief.

  • Runway margin is readable at a glance.
  • Fuel and W&B are tied to a selected aircraft.
  • Performance is presented as part of the decision, not a separate chore.

Flight record

Filed plans and aircraft records are still reachable.

BeBrief keeps filed plan status and aircraft setup near the preflight workflow so pilots can return to the record when needed.

  • Filed plan status and lifecycle actions are visible.
  • Aircraft hangar data stays connected to the active tail.
  • Records support the workflow without becoming the landing page headline.

Pilot control

Clear support, not replacement judgment.

BeBrief organizes preflight information so it is faster to inspect, repeat, and carry from the hangar to the airplane.

Data freshness Review what changed

Aircraft setup Tail-specific values matter

Aircraft templates require pilot customization against exact aircraft documents.
